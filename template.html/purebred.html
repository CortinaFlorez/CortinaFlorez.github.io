Purebred Dogs

Just like humans, dogs cant be traced back using their family tree. That is how we are able to classify purebreds. A dog may only be categorized as a purebred if its family has documented pedigree that it was the same breed for 3 generations or more. A pedigree is a breed that is recognized by a kennel club of having a pure family history.

What are some examples of purebred dogs?

  • French Bulldogs
  • Golden Retrievers
  • Poodles
  • Dachshunds
  • Rottweilers
  • German Shepards
