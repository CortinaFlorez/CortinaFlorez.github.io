Mixed breed or Mutts

Mutts or mixed breed are dogs that arent from one specific dog line and have been intentionally or unintentionally bred together. A lot of times this is done intentionally so they can embody characteristics or traits from the chosen breeds. Unlike a purebred, we usually cant completely trace back a mutts family history.

What are some examples of mixed breed dogs?

  • Golden Doodle
  • Pomsky
  • Cockapoo
  • Bernadoodle
  • Chihuahua
  • Puggle
